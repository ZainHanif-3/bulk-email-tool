# bulk-email-tool
Bulk Email Pro 📧 The ultimate tool for automating bulk email sequences via Outlook. Bulk Email Pro is a lightweight, local-first utility designed to help professionals and teams manage multiple email accounts without the constant headache of manual switching. By combining an intuitive web dashboard with the power of Outlook and Excel, it slashes the time spent on repetitive email tasks by up to 10 hours per week.  🚀 Key Features Unified Dashboard: Compose, edit, and organize email drafts in one centralized location.  Outlook Automation: Utilize existing Outlook credentials through a seamless, secure VBA-based integration.  Batch Processing: Export data to Excel and trigger bulk sends in one go.  Local-First Privacy: No cloud storage of your email credentials. Your data stays on your machine.  Multi-Account Support: Easily manage communications from multiple accounts without logging in and out.  🛠 How It Works Draft: Compose your emails (From, To, CC, BCC, Subject, Body) directly in the Bulk Email Pro interface.  Export: Click "Export to Excel" to generate a structured file with all your queued communications.  Execute: Open the generated file in Outlook, run the provided VBA macro (press Alt+F11), and watch as your emails are sent automatically in one go.  📋 Getting Started Prerequisites Microsoft Outlook (Desktop Application).  Excel (For processing the bulk export).  Setup Clone this repository to your local machine.  Open the web interface.  Compose your emails and click "Send All via Outlook" to download your Excel + VBA Macro file.  Follow the in-app instructions to import the .bas file into the Outlook VBA Editor.  Run the macro to begin your bulk send.  🛡 Security & Privacy Your security is our priority. Bulk Email Pro does not store your email passwords or transmit your messages to an external server. The actual sending process is handled locally by your own instance of Microsoft Outlook, ensuring your data remains private.  🤝 Contributing Contributions are welcome!
